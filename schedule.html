9 days of fun & strategy

The full camp schedule.

Day by day, here’s what your camper will be up to. We rotate subject blocks seamlessly so camp always stays fast-paced and exciting.

Day 1
✦ Intro

Orientation Day

Parents & Campers

Welcome orientation, campus walk-through, meet-the-counselors, and distribution of baseline materials.

Day 2

Science & English Block

Science

Cloud in a bottle experiment & general "lab" safety protocols.

English

Grammar fundamentals and parts of speech overview.

Day 3

Math & Art Block

Math

Area calculation exploration and multi-step word problem strategies.

Art

Basic geometric shapes, master color values, and clean coloring techniques.

Day 4

Science & English Block

Science

Interactive weather systems and cloud classification mapping.

English

Active reading text annotations workshop.

Day 5

Math & Art Block

Math

Simple fraction addition models.

Art

Color mixing, shading fundamentals, and live drawing tutorials.

Day 6

Science & English Block

Science

Cell Biology puzzle pieces building session.

English

Creative storytelling and narrative story writing.

Day 7
✦ Craft

Math & Tie-Dye Art Block

Math

Coordinate point graphing grids.

Art

Tie-Dye shirt creation! (Alternative low-mess arts available if needed).

Day 8

Science & English Block

Science

Organ puzzle systems, human anatomy maps, and corn germination necklaces.

English

Advanced grammar review, vocabulary development, and text structure.

Day 9
✦ Party!

Math, Crafts & Pizza Party

Math

Operations with like denominators and introduction to basic algebraic equations.

Art Rotation

Hands-on rotation day: Pony bead styling, pipe cleaner critters, and interactive Origami.

Celebration

Big Pizza Party, team awards, and closing camp ceremony!
