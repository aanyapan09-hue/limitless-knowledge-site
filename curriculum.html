Official curriculum

Everything your student will learn.

Four subjects, designed by students, built around hands-on activities. We seamlessly blend fun with knowledge so the program never feels like school.

📖

English

Topics overview: Parts of speech, sentence structures, tenses, and tone. We focus on expanding vocabulary through creative writing.

Grade 3

Topics
  • Simple sentence structure
  • Basic verb tenses
  • Avoiding fragments
Activities

Color-coded parts of speech, building sentences with word cards.

Grade 4

Topics
  • Compound sentences (FANBOYS)
  • Introduction to complex sentences
  • Tone & mood basics
Activities

"Find the run-on" challenges, vocabulary expansion journals.

Grade 5

Topics
  • Independent vs dependent clauses
  • Perfect tenses
  • Advanced vocabulary development
Activities

Transform simple → complex workshops, analyzing short stories.

Math

Visual learning is the core: pie charts for fractions, blocks for area, scavenger hunts for problem-solving, and stand-up board lessons.

Science

Students analyze data and use evidence to explain phenomena — from plant and animal structures to Earth's weather systems and human anatomy.

Cell Biology Puzzle Pieces

Paper, scissors, markers — build a cell.

The Grand Cell Tour

Introduction to what makes a cell tick.

Organ Paper Body Scramble

Reassemble the human body, organ by organ.

Weather Systems

Identify clouds and atmosphere with cotton & paper.

Spy Kids Day

Fingerprinting, forensic science, and observation skills.

Corn Germination Necklaces

Corn kernels, baggies, and damp paper towels.
